## TikTok → Instagram Repurpose Workflow

**The short answer: yes, repurpose — but the method matters by format.**

### Carousels (your primary format — use this)
TikTok carousels are image-based, not video. There is no watermark. The workflow:
1. Design slides in Canva → export as PNGs
2. Post to TikTok as carousel → wait 24–48h
3. Upload the same PNG files to Instagram as a carousel post (not a Reel)
4. Adapt the caption: remove TikTok-specific CTAs ("save this"), add 3–5 hashtags at the bottom
5. Done. ~15 min per carousel. No algorithm penalty.

Instagram carousels are shared in DMs peer-to-peer far more than Reels. For Phlow's content (informational, science-based) this is the right format for Instagram.

### Face-to-cam videos (use sparingly on IG)
- **Never repost a TikTok video with a TikTok watermark to Instagram.** Meta's algorithm explicitly deprioritizes it. This is confirmed in Meta's creator documentation.
- Option A (recommended): Film once, post the raw file to both platforms with different captions same day
- Option B: Use SnapTik (snaptik.app) — watermark-free download before reposting to Instagram
- Option C: Skip the IG repurpose for face-to-cam entirely. Face-to-cam does better on TikTok anyway (cold discovery). Instagram is warmer and carousels outperform there.

**Default rule:** Carousel = always repurpose. Face-to-cam = film raw and post to both, or skip Instagram.

### Caption adaptation for Instagram
| TikTok caption | Instagram adaptation |
|---|---|
| First line = hook (same) | First line = hook (same) |
| CTA: "→ phlow.work" | CTA: "link in bio" |
| 0–3 hashtags | Add: `#womenfounder #cycleawareness #productivitytips #womenshealth #biohacking` |
| No "tap to see" prompts (slides auto-play) | "swipe →" in caption or on slide 1 |

## Content Pillar Rotation

Six pillars. Three TikTok posts per week = 2 pillars per week + one repeat. Rotate to avoid staleness.

| Pillar | Code | Best for | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Day [X] Problem | **DAY** | Cold discovery, relatable crash moments | Carousel |
| User aha moments | **AHA** | Social proof, saves, shares | Carousel |
| Founder story | **FND** | Emotional connection, trust | Face-to-cam |
| The science | **SCI** | Credibility, saves from skeptics | Carousel |
| Myth busting | **MYTH** | Shares ("send this to someone") | Carousel |
| Phase-specific tips | **TIP** | Saves, bookmarks, action-oriented | Carousel |

**Rotation logic based on data:**
- Mid-luteal content = #1 in calendar taps → anchor every week with one DAY or SCI post touching days 17–25
- Ovulatory content = highest swipe volume → use TIP/SCI on ovulatory phase when you want reach
- Face-to-cam (FND) = 1x per week max, only for emotional/personal moments
- AHA posts = hold until you have 3+ real Phlow user quotes (do not manufacture them)

**Recommended weekly slot allocation:**
- Tuesday: MYTH or DAY (problem-first for cold audience)
- Thursday: SCI or TIP (depth for warm audience already following)
- Saturday: FND (emotional, personal — weekend audience has more time)
